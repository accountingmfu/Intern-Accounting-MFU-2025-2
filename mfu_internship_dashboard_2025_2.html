Key Observations
Data Highlights — AY 2025/2
Nearly half of all students (48.8%) secured placements at Big4 or mid-tier international accounting firms, led by Forvis Mazars, PwC, and Grant Thornton.
26.7% are at Thai private accounting companies — smaller domestic audit and accounting firms outside international networks, reflecting strong local industry absorption.
One in three students (32.6%) is placed at a multinational corporation, signalling solid international exposure for the accounting programme.
15.1% are in corporate companies (non-accounting firms: IT, hospitality, energy, logistics), indicating growing diversity beyond traditional accounting firm placements.
Listed company placements remain low at 3.5% (3 students at Thai Public Companies, non-accounting sector) — a potential area to expand industry partnership outreach.
Government & SOE placements account for 5.8% (5 students), including the Revenue Department and MFU's own CWIE unit.
